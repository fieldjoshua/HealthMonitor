Physical Symptoms Reporting

IMPORTANT - DO NOT USE:
• NO ALCOHOL at all (permanent restriction)
• NO NSAIDs (ibuprofen, naproxen, etc.)
• NO sedatives or opioids unless cleared by physician
• NO herbal supplements without doctor approval
Key Symptoms to Monitor:
• Itching - Mild: Occasional; Moderate: Frequent, distracting; Severe: Constant, intense
• Nausea - Mild: Occasional, no vomiting; Moderate: Frequent, some vomiting; Severe: Persistent, multiple episodes of vomiting
• Abdominal pain - Mild: Dull, intermittent; Moderate: Persistent, manageable; Severe: Intense, debilitating
• Swelling (legs/abdomen) - Mild: Slight, noticed only when looking; Moderate: Obvious, affecting movement; Severe: Significant, painful
• Yellowing of skin/eyes - Mild: Barely noticeable; Moderate: Visible; Severe: Deep yellow/orange tint
• Fatigue - Mild: Tiredness, manageable; Moderate: Limiting daily activities; Severe: Inability to perform basic tasks
• Loss of appetite - Mild: Reduced interest in food; Moderate: Skipping meals; Severe: No desire to eat for days
• Dark urine - Mild: Slightly darker than normal; Moderate: Tea-colored; Severe: Cola-colored
• Confusion - Mild: Occasional forgetfulness; Moderate: Frequent disorientation; Severe: Unable to recognize surroundings/people
• Bleeding/bruising - Mild: Small bruises; Moderate: Larger bruises, minor bleeding; Severe: Spontaneous bleeding, large bruises
